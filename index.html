Course Overview

The course is designed to introduce students to the fascinating concepts of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) in the context of life science applications. Throughout the course, students will explore the principles, theories, and practical applications of AI and ML, understanding how these cutting-edge technologies have revolutionized the life science domain. By integrating AI techniques with ML algorithms, students will learn how to build intelligent systems capable of tackling complex problems in the field. They will also gain expertise in data cleaning techniques, ensuring that the data used for training ML models is of the highest quality. The course will delve into clustering algorithms, enabling students to uncover hidden patterns and insights within life science datasets.

Pre-requisites

1. Knowledge of Data Structures: Students should be familiar with fundamental data structures, such as arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, and trees. They should understand the operations and applications of these data structures.

2.Proficiency in Python Language: Students are expected to have a working knowledge of the Python programming language. This includes understanding basic syntax, data types, control statements, functions, and object-oriented programming concepts.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Comprehend fundamental AI and ML concepts and techniques.
  2. Develop the ability to apply AI and ML to real-world challenges.
  3. Master languages like Python for ML and AI implementation.
  4. Acquire skills in data collection, cleaning, and preprocessing.
  5. Learn to integrate ML models into practical systems.
